Did you know that every time your dog barks, they’re caught in what experts call the “Bark Reflex Loop”?

Over time, this response builds into a habit that’s nearly impossible to break on your own. The Bark Reflex Loop keeps feeding itself, making your dog more reactive, anxious, and completely out of control.
